This file is the public index for non-code material distributed with AgriVerse. More
detailed source records and checksums remain beside the relevant assets. Inclusion here

choose that license separately.

## User-created Tripo model collection

The 23 character, vegetation, structure, and prop packages under
`unity/Assets/AgriVerse/Art/` were generated by the project owner through a paid Tripo
account using original prompts and references the owner had permission to use. The owner
authorized AgriVerse to modify, optimize, create derivatives from, and redistribute the
models and textures in the public source repository, compiled builds, demo video, and
hackathon submission, subject to Tripo's applicable terms.

The complete asset-ID list, original source FBX hashes, modification policy, and the
owner's verbatim rights confirmations are in
[`unity/Assets/AgriVerse/Art/ASSET-PROVENANCE.md`](unity/Assets/AgriVerse/Art/ASSET-PROVENANCE.md).
Source files are preserved beneath each asset's `Source/` directory; optimized meshes,
LODs, URP materials, colliders, and prefab wrappers are derivatives stored separately.

- Generator and applicable terms: Tripo AI, https://www.tripo3d.ai/terms
- Modification allowed: yes, under the owner's paid-account rights confirmation and
  applicable terms.
- Source redistribution allowed: yes, under that same confirmation and applicable terms.
- Compiled-build redistribution allowed: yes, under that same confirmation and applicable
  terms.
- Attribution required by this project: retain the provenance record and do not imply
  Tripo endorsement.

## Audio

The seven final Unity ambience and interaction clips under
`unity/Assets/AgriVerse/Art/Audio/` derive from recordings published by Joseph Sardin /
BigSoundBank as CC0/public domain on the preserved individual source pages.

- Source and license index: https://bigsoundbank.com/licenses.html
- Modification, source redistribution, and binary-build redistribution: permitted by the
  preserved source pages.
- Modifications: selected excerpts, loop crossfades, stereo/mono conversion, fades, and
  48 kHz delivery conversion.
- Required attribution: none under CC0; AgriVerse credits Joseph Sardin / BigSoundBank.
- Geographic limitation: the ambience is representative sound design and was not recorded
  in Vietnam.

Exact recording URLs, titles, source numbers, transformations, and hashes are retained in
[`Audio/SOURCE.md`](unity/Assets/AgriVerse/Art/Audio/SOURCE.md).

The web dialogue cue at `public/assets/audio/dialogue-pop.ogg` is
`confirmation_001.ogg` from Kenney's
[Interface Sounds](https://kenney.nl/assets/interface-sounds), distributed under
[CC0 1.0](https://creativecommons.org/publicdomain/zero/1.0/). It was renamed without
changing the audio.
