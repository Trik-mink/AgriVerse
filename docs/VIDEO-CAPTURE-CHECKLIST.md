# Video capture checklist

## Before recording

- [ ] Use the verified non-development universal release build.
- [ ] Set the display or player to 1920×1080 fullscreen.
- [ ] Confirm the public health endpoint reports ready without making a model call.
- [ ] Confirm the temporary judge service has not expired or exhausted its internal budget.
- [ ] Close Unity, terminals, email, chat, password managers, and unrelated browser tabs.
- [ ] Disable notifications and Focus-mode banners.
- [ ] Hide desktop files and personal account names.
- [ ] Never show `.env`, Render environment values, OpenAI keys, private dashboards, or
      `docs/BRAIN-HANDOFF.md`.
- [ ] Confirm microphone input and recording destination with a ten-second private test.
- [ ] Use headphones to prevent feedback.

## Exact gameplay path

1. Launch AgriVerse and hold on the orbital Field Network.
2. Rotate the globe; focus one incoming location.
3. Select Vietnam, type the player's name, and begin.
4. Complete Mai's arrival cue and walk to a test site.
5. Predict, collect the sample, reveal the reading, and open the Field Journal.
6. Interview one stakeholder and show a grounded response.
7. Cut to the completed three-person evidence state.
8. Submit a complete proposal at the planning table.
9. Advance the five-year consequence presentation.
10. Show grounded feedback and revise the proposal.
11. Show the original-versus-revised Future Walk.
12. Open the policy brief and the human-readable Judge View.
13. Show the named certificate, then Return to Field Network.

Controls: mouse drag/wheel and `Tab`/`Enter` on the globe; `WASD` and mouse in the world;
`E` to interact; `N` for the Field Journal; `Escape` to close or release the cursor.

## Recommended takes

- [ ] Take A: clean globe, Vietnam entry, and arrival.
- [ ] Take B: sample interaction and Field Journal.
- [ ] Take C: live stakeholder response.
- [ ] Take D: planning, Future Walk, feedback, revision, and comparison.

      presentation fixtures for retakes and label them accurately.

## Voiceover

- [ ] Target 2:35–2:50 total.
- [ ] Hook lands in the first three seconds.
- [ ] State the audience: high-school environmental science students.
- [ ] Say the full learning loop once.
- [ ] Name all four GPT-5.6 runtime systems.
- [ ] Explain grounding, structured validation, and citations briefly.
- [ ] Explain what Codex accelerated and what the human decided.
- [ ] Do not claim that incoming countries are playable.
- [ ] Do not read unsupported statistics from a mockup or reference image.

## Export and public upload

- [ ] Export H.264 MP4 at 1920×1080 with clear text and intelligible stereo audio.
- [ ] Watch the entire final export before upload.
- [ ] Confirm duration is under three minutes.
- [ ] Confirm no secret, private path, notification, or private note appears in any frame.
- [ ] Upload to public YouTube.
- [ ] Confirm incognito playback and public visibility.
- [ ] Add the final YouTube URL to README and Devpost.
- [ ] Do not use a placeholder URL in the submitted Devpost entry.
